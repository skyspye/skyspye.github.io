How It Works

Intelligence for every window seat

01 — ROUTE
Flight path computed

SkySpye models the actual flight corridor including takeoff, cruise, and descent — calculating the precise visibility window on each side of the aircraft at every point.

02 — DISCOVER
Landmarks identified

Every point of interest within the viewing corridor is identified — national parks, mountain ranges, city skylines, historic sites — enriched with Wikipedia context and imagery.

03 — SCORE
Moments ranked

Each landmark is scored by fame, angular prominence from altitude, real-time weather visibility, and phase of flight — surfacing the moments genuinely worth looking up for.

04 — DELIVER
Passenger informed

Passengers see exactly which window side to choose, what time to look, and what they'll see — before they book. Airlines embed this in their app, booking flow, or IFE system.
